Semester 2 Food & Beverage – Glion

This repository contains useful equations, formulas, forms, and reference materials for the Food & Beverage courses in Semester 2 at Glion.

The goal is to provide a centralized resource for students to quickly access key calculations, operational forms, and supporting documents used throughout the semester.

Contents
Food & Beverage equations and calculations
Costing and pricing formulas
Operational and management forms
Course reference materials
Study resources and notes
Purpose

This repository is designed to help Semester 2 students review essential Food & Beverage concepts, perform calculations accurately, and access commonly used forms and documents in one place.

This repository is intended as a study aid and should be used alongside official course materials provided by Glion.
